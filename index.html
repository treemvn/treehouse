About Me

Greetings, My name is Orlando. I grew up in the rural area of Northern Arizona on Dinétah (Navajo Nation) in the small town of Tsééhílį́ (Tsaile), I was immersed in a culture that instilled a deep appreciation for community, nature, and tradition. Being surrounded by nature and disconnected from the hustle and bustle of urban life gave me a unique perspective on the world, which I approach with the knowledge inherent in the culture and traditions of my people. This perspective has shaped my approach to life and motivate me to work hard to achieve success while staying true to my roots and values
